PHGOVONLINE — Delete Data

Information on how to request deletion of your personal data
Summary

PHGOVONLINE does not collect, store, or process personal data from users. If you wish to delete data you submitted, please contact the service provider (agency, office, or system) where you originally entered your information. They are the controller of your data and are the only party that can fulfill your deletion request.

Tip: Look for the provider’s official website or help desk. Use keywords like “privacy”, “data deletion”, or “contact us”.

Who should I contact?

Reach out to the government agency, office, or service whose form, portal, or app collected your data. Examples:

National government agency system where you submitted a form
Local government unit (LGU) online service or portal
State university/college registration platform
Attached agency or regional office online application

How to request deletion from a service provider

  1. Find the provider’s official website or support page.
  2. Prepare details to help them locate your record:
    • Full name and contact information used during submission
    • Approximate date/time of submission and the form or portal name
    • Any reference number, tracking code, or confirmation email
  3. Send a deletion request via their published channels (email, web form, help desk). Clearly state you are requesting erasure/deletion of your personal data.
  4. Keep a copy of your request and any acknowledgments for your records.

Frequently asked questions

Does PHGOVONLINE store any personal data?

No. PHGOVONLINE does not collect or store personal data and therefore cannot delete records it does not hold.

I don’t remember which provider collected my data. What can I do?

Search your email for confirmation messages or receipts around the date you submitted the information. Try keywords like the event or program name. You can also check browser history for the exact portal.

The provider hasn’t responded. Can PHGOVONLINE delete my data?

PHGOVONLINE has no access to providers’ databases. Please follow up with the provider through their official channels. If needed, you may escalate via their designated data protection or customer support contact.
